Cette mission de conseil intrinsèque du métier de consultant consiste à décrire une situation ou un problème, à rappeler les résultats qui en étaient espérés, à faire une analyse détaillée des divergences et très souvent à proposer des scénarios de convergence ou de correction de trajectoires. Dans certains cas, nos clients nous demandent de piloter les mises en œuvre des plans d’actions qui en sont issus.

Nous accompagnons nos clients dans toutes ou parties des phases induites par le choix de nouvelles solutions, d’applications logicielles, de solutions d’infrastructures, de chaines de production industrielle, de processus de gestion, de marchés publics, ...

Product Information Chain

Responsive design solutions and click and mortar require a reference and a single storage of all product information from different sources of the company (ERP, Marketing, CRM, Digital Asset Management,...). This information is taken in tasks and processes in different ways by the different processes composing the Product Information Chain.

Product Life Cycle Management is one of the sources of product data, focused on product innovation. It is used to design the product from the marketing brief to the final version and of the product. It hosts all the technical data of the product.

MDM tools support an active approach to critical control and protection in order to the company and common to several entities These tools cover several fields; the data customers, products, suppliers, raw materials,... Beyond the data cleaning aspect, Matching and golden record setting, they will strongly support governance, quality and data security processes. In addition, some PIM solutions, offer MDM features.

Product Information Management (PIM or GIP) is a technology that consists of centrally managing product information in order to to ensure an efficient and accurate distribution of this information across all channels- stores, web, mobile, terminals, social networks... Product Information Management (PIM or GIP) consists of provide an enriched, dynamic and centralized product repository to allow access to data and provide automated interfaces for data maintenance or consumption - article sheets, visuals, repositories, imports, exports, media production...
