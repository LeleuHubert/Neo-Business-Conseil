-------- Strategy & innovation --------

All sectors of activity are seeing their markets and ecosystems transform, products and uses must change and adapt flexibly to these rapid changes. New competitors emerging, the life cycles of products and services are profoundly modified and shaped must be completely rethought. To survive, it is necessary to rethink cyclically, or to significantly adapt initial strategies heavily impacted by these market changes. It is also a question of adapting the as accurately as possible the entire portfolio of products and services to the real ones constantly changing market demands. It is also a question of integrating more and more strong innovation processes in order to clearly differentiate itself from the competition.

The challenges of the general management will be of several kinds and will have to be applied to all the strategic and operational directions

  • The classic SWOT diagnosis of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats will be complemented by a matrix exclude, attenuate, reinforce, create, the whole built in a process out of the box
  • The questioning of traditional economic models and thought patterns with a view to look for breakthrough models
  • The instillation of a true culture of innovation and value in all departments

-------- Performance management --------

In an increasingly uncertain environment, characterized by strong competitive pressure, by rapid and structuring transformations coupled with an increasing complexity of the organizations, the company must anticipate and act ever more quickly. Managing performance means being able to ensure that the strategy is effectively implemented in operational actions and the successful achievement of the objectives set.

  • To convey a transversal and shared vision of the company's strategy
  • Develop a management model and indicators aligned with the organization and business model in place
  • Empower the various stakeholders on the defined objectives
  • Define the activities and resources to be implemented Insuffering a management culture within the organization

The management of the company's performance must be part of a global approach aimed at promote continuous improvement much more than control. NEO Business Conseil supports functional and business divisions in the definition and implementation of implementation of models and dashboards combining financial and operational performance in the form of a balanced scorecard also known as a "Balanced Scorecard". NEO Business Conseil's Data Intelligence expertise enables us to take advantage of the following new technologies for the implementation of agile and cross-functional management solutions.

our offers
