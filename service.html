-------- Strategy & innovation --------

All sectors of activity are seeing their markets and ecosystems transform, products and uses must change and adapt flexibly to these rapid changes. New competitors emerging, the life cycles of products and services are profoundly modified and shaped must be completely rethought. To survive, it is necessary to rethink cyclically, or to significantly adapt initial strategies heavily impacted by these market changes. It is also a question of adapting the as accurately as possible the entire portfolio of products and services to the real ones constantly changing market demands. It is also a question of integrating more and more strong innovation processes in order to clearly differentiate itself from the competition.

The challenges of the general management will be of several kinds and will have to be applied to all the strategic and operational directions

  • The classic SWOT diagnosis of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats will be complemented by a matrix exclude, attenuate, reinforce, create, the whole built in a process out of the box
  • The questioning of traditional economic models and thought patterns with a view to look for breakthrough models
  • The instillation of a true culture of innovation and value in all departments

-------- Digital transformation --------

Induced by new technologies, digital and/or digital transformations refer to the process which allows the integration of technologies that are designed to improve the customer experience as well as to rationalize production costs In all cases, it is a question of increasing value for the company as well as for its user in a seven-point approach; Which underlie the search for potential axes of rupture

  • 1) technological
  • 2) co-creativity (design thinking, focus group,...)
  • 3) open innovation
  • 1) definition of the vision by clarifying the issues
  • 2) training of employees
  • 3) Restructuring/recasting the organization towards a horizontal and collaborative dimension
  • 4) Working in collaborative mode
  • 5) Positioning the customer at the heart of the strategy
  • 6) Analysis and use of data to identify customer needs
  • 7) Collection / Testing / Progress
